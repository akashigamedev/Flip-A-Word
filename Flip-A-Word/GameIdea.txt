card flip game
uses word api
every card has description, word count, award count in
the front and the complete word, it's example in a sentence
 and the recieved award stars in the back.

player will choose from four option where only one is 
correct, once the choice is made the card will flip
to reveal the correct answer.

based on how long it took them to answer they will 
recieve stars, under 15 seconds - three stars, 30 seconds
, - 2 stars, 1 minute - 1 star.

the game is endless and gets progressively hard.
the game requires an internet connection to play.
we will show ads on this game.
